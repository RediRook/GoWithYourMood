Go With Your Mood is an interactive website created by The Marshmallows that is targeted towards international students newly arrived to Australia. Go With Your Mood provides a means for people unfamiliar with their surroundings to find places nearby that can help ease them into their new lives in Australia. We do this by by incorporating the mood and location of a user to find suitable places nearby that could help.

The activities suggested range from visiting restaurants, sporting events, entertainment places, going on long drives, to public events, and many other things. We aim to help you find something that can make you feel a little better in your new home.

Team The Marshmallows would really appreciate your feedback so please provide ratings to our suggestion and tell us about your experience by them as well.
